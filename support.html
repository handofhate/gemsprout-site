Common Questions

How do I add my family?

When you first open GemSprout, a family code is automatically created for you. Share that code with other family members so they can join from their device under Settings → Join a Family.

How do I reset my data?

You can permanently delete all family data by going to Settings → Reset All Data inside the app. This cannot be undone.

I lost my family code — what do I do?

Your family code is always visible in Settings → Family Code. If you no longer have access to any device with the app installed, contact us and we'll do our best to help.

Something isn't working right.

In the app, go to Settings → Send Debug Log to email us a diagnostic report. This helps us identify and fix issues faster.
