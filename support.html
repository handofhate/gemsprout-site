Common Questions

How do I add my family?

Your family code is created when you first set up GemSprout. Kids join by entering the family code on their device. If you're inviting a second parent or partner, they sign in with their own Google or Apple account — they don't need the family code. Once they're in, they'll have full parent access.

How do I reset my data?

You can permanently delete all family data by going to Settings → Reset All Data inside the app. This cannot be undone.

I lost my family code — what do I do?

Your family code is always visible in Settings → Family Code. Keep in mind the family code is primarily for kids to join — parents can always sign back into the parent dashboard using the same Google or Apple account they originally used. If you're still having trouble, contact us and we'll help.

Something isn't working right.

Email us at support@gemsprout.com and describe what's happening. Screenshots are always helpful. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.
